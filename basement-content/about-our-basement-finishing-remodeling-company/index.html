About Our Basement Remodeling Company

Custom Integrated Designs

Take Your Basement To A New Level™

Choose Custom Integrated Designs. Ltd. (CID) for any remodeling or home upgrade you have planned.

We specialize in basement finishing projects accented by custom cabinetry, eye-catching architectural details, and unique floor plans, but we also are the premier contractor for home repair, roof replacement, and more.

On every project we provide uncompromised attention to detail and personalized service.

Communication and customer service are a main focus of our basement remodeling company, with key personnel available to help with any questions and decisions that need to be made, even during the evening hours until 8:00 p.m. and on Saturdays.

Having been in business for over 35 years, we realize that when someone is working in your home for an extended period of time, that it is very important to solve problems and respond quickly to customer needs.

Offering Personalized Service and Attention to Detail since 1976 in Denver, Aurora, Castle Rock, Castle Pines areas, Centennial, Elizabeth, Englewood, Greenwood Village, Highlands Ranch, Kiowa, Littleton, Parker, and Sedalia.

Company History

Company founder and General Manager, Gerald Vidmar started CID in 1975 in his parents garage at 16 years old. Building furniture and cabinetry for friends, neighbors, and family, he knew what he would do for the rest of his life and enjoyed every aspect of this business.

Having always been interested in architecture and construction, Jerry quickly found a niche in kitchen and bathroom remodeling, using his handmade, custom-designed cabinets. Within a few years, a new concept of adding valuable living space in the basements of homes began to take off.

CID was poised to be one of the metro areas first basement remodeling companies at the ground level of a brand new industry. The custom cabinetry that Jerry was building quickly became a calling card, and a unique offering for people that were ready to finish their basements or remodel their current living space.

Today, Custom Integrated Designs is still the only basement remodeling company that can offer custom wet bars, entertainment centers, and other custom built-ins directly from its own woodshop.

President: Gerald Vidmar
