Clean Modern Parker Basement

This New Level basement project in Parker CO is inspired by clean modern styling, cabinetry and finish details.

Project Details - Open Bar

Under stair space is used to add cabinetry and wine and beverage coolers keeping the bar area open and spacious. The open bar design provides the owner with a large comfortable area for cocktails and entertaining.

With the open style bar area family and friends can sit or stand at the bar for conversation or game play.

The custom bar cabinetry, built in our own wood shop provides unique one of a kind style, personality and quality. The back lighted, floating display shelves are clean and elegant. The area in front of the bar is large enough for a future pool table.

Project Details - Bathroom

The bathroom is complete with a large custom built vanity cabinet and solid surface counter top.

All custom cabinetry built for our projects feature solid wood doors, drawer fronts and face frames with strong high grade Baltic Birch drawer construction and self closing drawer glides along with concealed European style door hinges.

The shower with a classy subway tile layout is comfortable and warm. A large guest bedroom provides the family with room for out of town guests. A large walk in closet in the bedroom provides storage for guests as well as bonus space for winter clothes or sports equipment for the family.

The large cove off to the side of the bar area provides an area to tuck in a couple of pieces of exercise equipment and a study/play area for the youngsters of the family. This area is neatly disguised behind short knee walls with caps that keep any disorder hidden from the main area of the basement without making the area feel cramped or disconnected from the rest of the finished space.

A declarative column detail which covers the structural column support neatly delineates the game and bar space from the media area. This leaves the area feeling larger and allows for the entire entertainment area to have views of the television for gatherings to watch sporting events.

The colors, textures finishes and other stylings were inspired by Heather Gale Interiors. Heather works with CID basements to help create unique and personalized style for your new space. Call Jerry today to create your "New Level"
