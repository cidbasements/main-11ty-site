Contemporary Basement with a Vintage Baseball Theme In Aurora CO

Our recently finished basement project in Aurora CO was finished ahead of schedule in time for the home owner’s annual Halloween celebration. This basement features the latest trend in cabinetry style with modern Shaker style doors featuring self-closing drawers and soft close door hinges custom built in our custom wood shop. With the capability of providing custom cabinetry at competitive prices CIDbasements.com continues to be a style leader in the industry for those looking for more than just the average cookie cutter basement. For those that are willing to put just a little more effort into their “New Level” CID ltd. can provide these custom aspects in just a couple more weeks’ time over the blow and go schedules being offered by some cookie cutter basement finishers. The time taken to hand build custom cabinetry and architectural details sets every CID ltd. basement apart from the average.

With a uniquely styled custom bar and one of a kind counter tops featuring barn wood tile tops with real barn wood edge details gives the bar area the Vintage Baseball feel that the owner was looking for. Built in beer dispensers hidden from view behind the scene and space for a vintage style refrigerator and neon bar lights. Custom media and theater systems custom tailored to fit any size theater area. Viewing counters set up to maximize the seating capacity of an otherwise smaller space is a good example of how CID basements takes the time to come up with unique features that will add value and quality to your project.

Custom built vanity and counter tops along with stylish tile in the shower and on the floor makes the bathroom a stand out feature of the basement. It may be difficult to get your guests to leave!

Let us use our expertise to design a floor plan that will maximize the use of every square foot of your basement to gain its full potential. Areas incorporated to be flex spaces that can be multi tasked for future mixed use optimizes the sustained value of the money spent on your project. With over 40 years of experience in custom cabinetry and interior design, Jerry Vidmar will work closely with you to give you the features and style you are looking for.
