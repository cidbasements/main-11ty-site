Stylized Modern Basement with Unique detail in Castle Rock CO

This unique basement located in Castle Rock Colorado blends a unique combination of slick modern stylings along with unique and one of a kind details. Walking down the stairs from the main level starts the journey of this unique “New Level”. The children’s elaborate under stairs play house starts with an entry off of the staircase with a doorway to the second level of this awesome play space. A ladder from this level takes the kids play area the rest of the way down to the main floor of this cute and custom play space under the stairs. It is complete with multiple hidden exits and entries including a tunnel exiting into the kids climbing wall and play area. The front of the play house is complete with a faux roofline depicting a cute little cabin in the woods. The mountain and lodge feel is carried on with what may be the most unique feature of this basement. Kids and adults as well will find a thrilling ride down the twisting turning mine shaft slide that starts on the main floor laundry area and exits through the “Gold Mine” shaft next to the kids learning area along with a custom mural depicting a beautiful cabin in the mountains along with a dry erase and chalk board hidden behind. A custom service bar is set to the side of the play house for entertaining. The entire basement is filled with unique niche areas for art display and memorabilia. The media area boasts a custom media storage cabinet with sleek modern lines.

A guest bedroom provides a warm and comforting place to visit and enjoy photos of friends and family displayed along two walls of decorative knee wall cap shelves. A beautiful modern and warm ¾ bathroom with an oversize tile shower with seating and built in shampoo niches may make it hard for out of town guests to leave. The bathroom is complete with a custom furniture style vanity with quartz counter top. Custom dimensional stone and tile details make this bathroom one of a kind.

The main area is finished off with a full wall book case that includes a swinging hidden doorway behind the end cabinet of the bookcase. This hides the entry into the home owner’s large home office space.

All of the cabinetry and unique architectural details have been handcrafted either in CID basements custom wood shop or on site. These custom details set this basement finish apart and make this home a stand out in the neighborhood.
