With home values at all time lows homeowners are investing in their existing homes instead of purchasing new or larger homes.

Homeowners are discovering that they can add real value to their existing homes by adding usable living space in their basements or upgrading kitchens and bathrooms.

With the added space of a finished basement or the added convenience of a new kitchen or bathroom many people discover that they can stay in their home longer even with growing families.

When homeowners decide to move into a different home, these investments and improvements in their homes make it easier to sell there home in a very competitive home market.
