This beautiful “New Level” in Parker Colorado was inspired by the home owner’s years of living in Fairbanks Alaska. This custom man cave features a full service sit up wet bar area with beautiful hand built custom cabinetry built in CIDs very own custom wood shop. The Knotty Alder cabinets with a deep rich Cherry inspired stain are a focal point of this beautiful new living space. The bar features two built in refrigerators for beverage storage and a built in micro wave for food warming and popcorn during the big game. Custom features include wine storage as well as concealed and lockable liquor storage with fold back pocket doors. The large column details give the bar a feeling of being in an old time Saloon in the Rocky Mountains. The bar is centrally located allowing for people to mingle about the bar area while watching a rousing game of pool or enjoy good conversation with a great view of the large television on the wall in the sitting area. Media equipment is set up in a cabinet that backs under the stairs for easy access to the wiring of the media and audio systems which feature complete surround sound for the recreation area and television. Plenty of room was designed into the basement for a regular size pool table with additional game or pub table seating nearby. Guests will enjoy the comfortable garden level bedroom with lots of natural light. A ¾ guest bathroom right outside their door featuring a custom tile surround and glass shower door. The bathroom is completed with custom built vanity including soft closing drawers and doors. Custom designed to maximize the counter top space for guests. Near the stairs and the bar a finished storage room was finished for the possible future addition of a wine cellar. The stairs were opened up with a balusters and railing to make the basement feel like another level and not a basement.
